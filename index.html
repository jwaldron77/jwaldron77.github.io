Dalton Wrestling Mission Statement:

The Dalton Wrestling program will serve K-12th grade of the Dalton/Kidron area. Our goal is to help develop well-rounded young people who will learn the fundamentals and importance of responsibility, sportsmanship, academics and community pride.
